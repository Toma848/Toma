About Me

Hello! I'm Farhana Akter Toma, a Fine Arts student at Rajshahi University. My work explores the intersection of identity, emotion, and nature through various mediums such as oil painting, watercolor, and digital illustration.

Art has always been my escape and my voice. With every stroke of my brush, I try to tell stories that words often fail to express. I draw inspiration from my surroundings, dreams, and heritage to create expressive pieces.
